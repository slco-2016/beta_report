Revised Hypothesis

Supervision Clients are juggling a lot - like finding and keeping a job, staying sober, taking care of their kids, and keeping a roof over their heads. Maintaining consistent, open communication with their case managers is key to navigating all of this while completing supervision successfully.

We learned that some people are indeed simply forgetting their appointments, but there are other social, emotional, financial, logistical, and institutional barriers that account for people failing to appear at court and comply with their supervision.
