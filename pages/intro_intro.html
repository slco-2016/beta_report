Challenge

Salt Lake County is struggling with people failing to show for court appearances and other appointments required for them to stay in compliance with the justice system.

This wastes the time of judges, court officers, police officers, as well as, the money spent on their services. For individuals going through the justice system, failing to appear results in the issuance of a bench warrant. With a bench warrant issued, the next time that person comes in contact with police, even for a minor violation like a traffic ticket, they are arrested immediately and taken to jail.
